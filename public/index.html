About AcadScore
AcadScore is a free, professional academic and career utility platform built for Indian students, graduates, banking aspirants, and working professionals.

Our mission is to provide accurate, fast, and easy-to-use calculators that help students track their academic performance, prepare for job applications, and manage their finances. All calculations happen entirely in your browser — no data is stored or sent to any server.
